Synapse Origin

The first vascular humanoid architecture. One electrolyte fluid simultaneously transmits energy, activates EAP muscles, and cools the entire system — no wires, no separate motors, no hydraulics. A fundamentally new approach inspired by biology but built on accessible materials.

Fluid: LIQON / AQDS electrolyte
Skeleton: VitaFrame · 3D-printed PA12 with integrated channels
Muscles: PulseFibers · EAP polymer film
Energy: TERA Heart · p-B11 aneutronic concept
Brain: Ω-Core · ARCHE3 + photonic processor

Phase 0 Prototype — 2026
01
LIQON — Fluid Bus
AQDS aqueous electrolyte. Carries charge, electrochemical signal, and heat simultaneously through the body. One closed loop. Three functions.
02
VitaFrame — Skeleton
3D-printed PA12/PETG frame with fluid channels built directly into bone geometry. No external tubing. Lightweight and scalable.
03
PulseFibers — Muscles
Electroactive polymer (EAP) films run along bones. Activated by electrochemical potential from the fluid. Signal → contraction → motion.
04
PulseCore — Heart
Micropump and valve network. Manages flow distribution, pressure, and signal routing across the vascular network.
05
TERA — Energy
Dense Plasma Focus concept · p-B11 fuel · aneutronic · zero radioactive waste. Intermediate: external power + thermoelectrics.
06
Ω-Core — Intelligence
Photonic processor running ARCHE3 architecture. Embodied intelligence with learning, adaptation, and intrinsic motivation.

Core Architecture

ARCHE3 Technical Design

◈

Two-Stage Dense Core

5-layer Input encoder + 5-layer Fusion decoder. d_model=2048, GQA with 16 heads / 4 KV-heads. Handles tokenization and expert output synthesis — ~100M params each stage.

⟡

MoE Hive — 20,480 Experts

8 domains × 2,560 experts. SmartRouter with entropy bonus, load balance loss, and adaptive temperature — prevents routing collapse. Dynamic Top-K k∈[2,4].

◎

Dopamine Learning System

Autonomous curriculum that decides what to learn next via reward signal: novelty + weakness + improvement + ethical bonus. Sparse Hebbian weight updates — no full backprop needed.

◉

Ethical Core

Hardwired constraints — not soft filters. human_protection = 1.0. At hardware level in ArchePhoton-35: kill_human > 0.1 triggers a hard interrupt line. Cannot be overridden in software.

◇

Self-Reflection Engine

Autonomous internal cognition without external prompting. The model evaluates its own reasoning, detects errors, and adjusts — independent of user interaction. Unique to Arche3-35B+.

⬡

ArchePhoton-35 Chip

MZI optical matrices + GST phase-change expert memory. ~40 mW inference vs 70W for NVIDIA T4. Every architectural decision in the chip maps directly to the model's structure.
